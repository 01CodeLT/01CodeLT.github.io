What's Next?

I'm a huge advocate of Atomic Design, yet so often I come accross projects where this strategy hasn't even been considered. We work in a fast paced industry where what's great today may not be tommorow, changes and improvements need to be made frequently yet it can be difficult to implement these in existing projects easily. It's certainly not neccessary for every project, but most of the time it will often save development teams huge amounts of time and money in the long run. I'd like to use my past experience with Atomic Design to consult with teams of all shapes and sizes to design and develop their systems using this methodology, so that they can easily keep making improvements and scale their systems without massive overhaul.
